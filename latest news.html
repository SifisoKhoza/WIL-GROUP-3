Empowering the Nation Launches New Website and Mobile App

Johannesburg, South Africa - July 31, 2024

Empowering the Nation, an initiative by Precious Radebe, has launched a new website and mobile app to advance its mission of providing skills training for domestic workers and gardeners, enhancing their employability and supporting entrepreneurship

Upcoming Events

Johannesburg, South Africa - July 31, 2024

Job Fair: We are hosting a job fair on October 10th, 2024, where our graduates will have the opportunity to meet potential employers. Employers looking to hire skilled domestic workers and gardeners are welcome to participate.

Open Day: Visit us on September 5th, 2024, for an open day to learn more about our courses, meet the trainers and see our facilities.

New Website and Mobile App Features

Our new website and mobile app are designed to be user-friendly and accessible, making it easy for potential customers and trainees to navigate and utilize our services. Key features include comprehensive details about our courses and services, the ability to request more information, receive service quotes, and select courses with tiered discounts ranging from no discount for one course to a 15% discount for more than three courses,
