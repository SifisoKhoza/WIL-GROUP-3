Sewing - 6 month course


Fees:R1500


The Sewing Course is designed to provide participants with fundamental skills needed to perform garment alterations and create new, tailored clothing. This Course will empower individuals to offer professional sewing services, enhancing their employability or enabling them to start their own small businesses.



  • Study material and Shipping
  • Study anytime
  • Certified by Sewing Center
  • Dedicated support team
  • 6 Month Duration


Purpose:


To provide alterations and new garment tailoring services. By the end of the course, individuals will have the confidence and competence to work with a variety of fabrics and sewing techniques.


Content:

  • Types of Stitches:
    • Learning different types of hand and machine stitches (e.g., straight stitch, zigzag stitch, blind hem stitch).
    • Understanding which stitches are appropriate for different fabrics and sewing tasks.
    • Practicing stitch techniques for durability and aesthetic finish.
  • Threading a Sewing Machine:
    • Step-by-step guidance on threading a sewing machine properly.
    • Familiarization with machine parts and functions.
    • Troubleshooting common machine issues such as thread tension problems and jams.
  • Sewing Buttons, Zips, Hems, and Seams:
    • Techniques for attaching buttons and sewing buttonholes.
    •Inserting zippers accurately and neatly into garments. Hemming trousers, skirts, and dresses for a professional finish.
    •Strengthening seams to ensure long-lasting garment construction.
  • Alterations:
    • Performing basic garment alterations such as taking in or letting out seams, shortening or lengthening hems, and adjusting waistbands.
    • Fitting clothes to suit different body shapes and sizes.
    •Understanding fabric types and how they affect alteration methods
  • Designing and Sewing New Garments:
    • Basics of garment design, including taking measurements and drafting simple patterns.
    •Selecting appropriate fabrics and cutting them accurately.
    •Assembling and sewing garments from start to finish, following design specifications.

This course is ideal for individuals who wish to enhance their sewing skills, whether for personal use, employment or entrepreneurial ventures. Participants will gain the ability to perform a wide range of sewing tasks, from simple repairs to creating custom garments, positioning them as valuable assets in the household or marketplace.
