Garden Maintenance - 6 week course


Fees:R750


The Garden Maimenance course is designed to equip participants with essential skills and knowledge for maintaining a domestic garden. This course focuses on effective watering, pruning and planting techniques to ensure a healthy and thriving-garden. To provide basic knowledge of watering, pruning and planting in a domestic garden. The course is designed to equip participants with essential skills.



  • Study material and Shipping
  • Study anytime
  • Certified by Garden Maintenance Training Center
  • Dedicated support team
  • 6 Week Duration

Purpose:


To provide basic knowledge of watering, pruning and planting in a domestic garden.By the end of the course, individuals will be able to maintain a garden effectively, promoting plant health and sustainability.



Content:

  • Water Restrictions and the Watering Requirements of Indigenous and Exotic Plants:
    • Learning about the different watering needs of indigenous (native) plants versus exotic (non-native) plants.
    • Understanding local water restrictions and how they impact garden maintenance.
    •Techniques for efficient watering, including drip irrigation and rainwater harvesting, to conserve water while keeping plants healthy.
  • Pruning and Propagation of Plants:
    • Learning when and how to prune different types of plants to encourage healthy growth and flowering Understanding the importance of pruning for plant shape, health, and disease prevention.
    • Basic techniques for plant propagation, including cutting, layering, and division to create new plants from existing ones.
  • Planting Techniques for Different Plant Types:
    • Learning how to plant various types of plants, including shrubs, flowers, trees, and ground cover.
    • Understanding soil preparation, plant spacing, and root care for optimal growth.
    • Tips for planting in different seasons and ensuring proper planting depth and aftercare for new plants.

This course is ideal for individuals who want to enhance their gardening skills, whether for personal gardening or to offer basic garden maintenance services. Participants will ain practical knowledge in essential garden care techniques, helping them create and maintain a healthy, beautiful garden
