First Aid - 6 month course


Fees:R1500


The First Aid course is designed to provide participants with essential first aid awareness and basic life support skills. This course equips individusls with the knowledge and practical abilities to handle medical emergencies confidently. Here's a breakdown of the key elements.



  • Study material and Shipping
  • Study anytime
  • Certified by First Aid Center
  • Dedicated support team
  • 6 Month Duration


Purpose:


To provide first aid awareness and basic life support. By the end of the course, individuals will be able to respond to common injuries and emergency situations effectively, ensuring they can provide immediate assistance before professional help arrives.


Content:

  • Wounds and Bleeding:
    •Understanding different types of wounds (cuts, abrasions, punctures).
    • Techniques to manage bleeding and prevent infection.
    • Applying pressure bandages and dressings.
  • Burns and Fractures:
    • Recognizing and treating different degrees of burns (first, second, and third-degree).
    • Handling fractures and immobilizing injured areas using splints and other aids.
  • Emergency Scene Management:
    •Assessing the scene for safety before approaching the injured person.
    • Prioritizing medical care and managing multiple casualties.
    • Basic knowledge of when to call for professional medical help.
  • Cardio-Pulmonary Resuscitation (CPR):
    • Learning the steps of CPR for both adults and children.
    • Understanding the correct chest compression techniques and breath ratios.
    • Using CPR to maintain blood flow and oxygenation in cases of cardiac arrest.
  • Respiratory Distress (e.g., Choking, Blocked Airway):
    •Recognizing signs of choking or a blocked airway.
    •Techniques such as the Heimlich maneuver for clearing airway obstructions.
    •Providing assistance to individuals experiencing breathing difficulties.

This course is suitable for individuals looking to upskill for personal knowledge, workplace safety, or to be better prepared in their roles as domestic workers, caregivers, or gardeners. Participants who complete this course will gain valuable skills to address emergencies effectively, potentially saving lives.
