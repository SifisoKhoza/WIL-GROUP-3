Landscaping - 6 month course


Fees:R1500


The Landscaping course, priced at R1500, is designed to equip participants with the knowledge and skills necessary to provide professicnal landscaping services for both new and established gardens. This course focuses an enhancing the aesthetic and functional qualities of outdoor spaces through plant selection garden design, and the incorporation of fixed structures.



  • Study material and Shipping
  • Study anytime
  • Certified by Landscaping Center
  • Dedicated support team
  • 6 Month Duration


Purpose:


To provide landscaping services for new and established gardens. By the end of the course, individuals will be able to offer landscaping services, improving the appearance and functionality of gardens through plant arrangements and the integration of various elements like fountains, benches, and statues.


Content:

  • Indigenous and Exotic Plants and Trees:
    • Identifying a range of indigenous (native) and exotic (non-native) plants and trees.
    • Understanding the growing conditions required for different species.
    • Learning the environmental impact and benefits of using indigenous plants for water conservation and supporting local wildlife.
  • Fixed Structures (Fountains, Statues, Benches, Tables, Built-in Braai):
    • Incorporating fixed structures into a garden design to enhance its visual appeal and functionality.
    • Planning the placement of features like fountains, statues, benches, tables, and builit-in braais to complement the natural elements Understanding materials, installation techniques, and the long-term maintenance of these structures.
  • Balancing of Plants and Trees in a Garden:
    • Achieving balance and harmony between different plants and trees.
    • Considering factors such as plant height, spread, and growth rates to ensure proportionality.
    • Creating a balanced garden layout that promotes plant health and longevity.
  • Aesthetics of Plant Shapes and Colors:
    • Selecting plants based on their shapes, sizes, and color schemes to create visually appealing arrangements.
    • Understanding how plant textures and forms can complement one another in a garden design.
    • Designing seasonal garden displays by choosing plants that bloom or change colors throughout the year.
  • Garden Layout:
    • Planning garden layouts that maximize space, functionality, and aesthetic appeal.
    • Creating pathways, flower beds, and plant groupings that guide the eye and movement through the garden.
    • Understanding drainage, soil types, and sunlight exposure to design gardens that thrive in different environments.

This course is perfect for individuals looking to offer professional landscaping services or improve their own garden designs. Participants will ain the skils needed to create balanced, beautiful, and functional outdoor spaces, making them highly marketable in the landscaping industry.
