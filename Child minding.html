Child Minding - 6 week course


Fees:R750


The Child-Minding course is aimed at providing participants with fundamental knowledge and skills to care for babies and young children. The course covers various stages of child development, focusing on understanding and meeting the needs of chilidren from birth to toddler age, ensuring they are nurtured and supported in a safe environment.



  • Study material and Shipping
  • Study anytime
  • Certified by Child Minding Center
  • Dedicated support team
  • 6 Week Duration

Purpose:


To provide basic child and baby care. By the end of the course, individuals will be prepared to provide proper care and support for infants and toddlers, ensuring their physical, emotional, and developmental needs are met.



Content:

  • Birth to Six-Month-Old Baby Needs:
    • Understanding the care requirements for newborns, including feeding, diapering, and sleep routines.
    • Learning techniques for soothing and comforting babies, including safe swaddling.
    • Basic health and hygiene practices such as bathing and changing nappies.
    • Monitoring baby's growth and developmental milestones during the early months.
  • Seven-Month to One-Year-Old Needs:
    • Recognizing the developmental changes as babies become more mobile (e.g.., crawing, standing).
    • Introducing solid foods and managing the transition from milk to a balanced diet.
    • Ensuring baby safety as they begin exploring their environment.
    • Encouraging early cognitive and motor skill development through interaction and play.
  • Toddler Needs: Understanding t the needs of toddlers (ages 1-3), focusing on physical activity, nutrition, and emotional well- being:
    • Managing tantrums and fostering positive behavioral development.
    • Assisting with toilet training and establishing routines for, sleep, meals, and playtime.
    • Encouraging social interaction and independence in a safe and structured environment.
  • Educational Toys:
    • Choosing age-appropriate toys that support a child's physical, cognitive, and emotional development.
    • Understanding how play promotes learning and identifying toys that stimulate creativity, problem-solving, and coordination.
    • Creating a play environment that is safe and conducive to growth and exploration.

This course is ideal for individuals who want to improve their child-care skills, whether for professional child- minding roles or for taking care of their own children, It provides practical knowledge to ensure that the unique needs of children from infancy to toddlerhood are met, helping them thrive in a nurturing and stimulating environment.
