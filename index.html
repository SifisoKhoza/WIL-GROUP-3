ABOUT US

Established in 2018, Empowering the Nation offers courses in Johannesburg, where hundreds of domestic workers and gardeners have been trained. Participants can choose between six-month Learnerships and six-week Short Skills Training Programs. These programs are designed to empower individuals by enhancing their skills, making them more marketable in the job market.



6 - WEEK COURSES

Accredited

Empowering the Nation offers six-week short courses to quickly build essential skills. The Child Minding course focuses on child development and safety, the Cooking course covers culinary techniques from basic to advanced, and the Garden Maintenance course teaches practical garden care. Enroll now to enhance your skills and professional profile.



6 - MONTH COURSES

Accredited

Empowering the Nation offers six-month courses to equip domestic workers and gardeners with valuable skills. Courses include First Aid for emergency response, Sewing for garment creation and alteration, Landscaping for garden design and maintenance, and Life Skills for personal and professional development. Enroll today to enhance your skills and boost your career prospects.
