Cooking - 6 week course


Fees:R750


The Cooking course is designed to teach participants how to prepare and cook nutritious meals for families. The course focuses on understanding nutritional needs, meal planning and the practical skillsneeded to prepare a variety of healthy, balanced meals.



  • Study material and Shipping
  • Study anytime
  • Certified by Culinary Center
  • Dedicated support team
  • 6 Week Duration


Purpose:


To prepare and cook nutritious family meals. By the end of the course, individuals will be equipped to plan, prepare, and cook meals that meet the dietary needs of a healthy family, ensuring both variety and nutrition in everyday meals.


Content:


  • Nutritional Requirements for a Healthy Body:
    • Understanding the basic nutritional needs for different age groups (children, adults, and seniors).
    • Learning about essential nutrients like vitamins, minerals, and fiber, and their role in maintaining health.
    • Identifying how different foods contribute to energy, immunity, and overall well-being.
  • Types of Protein, Carbohydrates, and Vegetables:
    • Exploring various sources of protein (meat, poultry, fish, legumes, and plant-based options) and their health benefits.
    • Understanding different types of carbohydrates (complex vs. simple) and choosing healthier options like whole grains and fiber-rich foods.
    • Learning about a variety of vegetables and their role in a balanced diet, including seasonal and nutrient- dense choices.
  • Planning Meals:
    • How to plan weekly meals that are nutritious, varied, and budget-friendly.
    • Balancing meals to include appropriate portions of proteins, carbohydrates, and vegetables.
    • Incorporating snacks and meal prep techniques to save time and reduce food waste.
    • Customizing meal plans to accommodate dietary preferences or restrictions (e.g., vegetarian, low-carb, etc.).
  • Preparation and Cooking of Meals:
    • Practical skills for preparing ingredients, including cutting, marinating, and seasoning.
    • Learning different cooking methods (baking, boiling, steaming, grilling, sautéing) to maximize nutritional value.
    • Creating delicious and nutritious meals using simple, everyday ingredients.
    • Safe food handling, storage, and maintaining kitchen hygiene.

This course is ideal for individuals who want to improve their cooking skils and ensure that they can prepare healthy, balanced meals for their families, Participants will gain the knowledge and confidence needed to plan and cook meals that support good health and well-being, while also being enjoyable to eat.
