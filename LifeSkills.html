Life Skills - 6 month course


Fees:R1500


The Life Sklls course aims to equip participants with essential skills to navigate everyday life more effectively. The course focuses on practical knowledge that empowers indrviduals to handle basic financial, legal, and literacy-related tasks with confdence and independence.



  • Study material and Shipping
  • Study anytime
  • Certified by Life Skills Center
  • Dedicated support team
  • 6 Month Duration


Purpose:


To provide skills to navigate basic life necessities. Upon completion, individuals will have the knowledge and tools to handle everyday tasks more effectively, enhancing their personal and professional lives.


Content:

  • Opening a Bank Account:
    • Understanding the different types of bank accounts (savings, checking, fixed deposit).
    •Learning how to apply for and open a bank account.
    • Managing bank accounts, including deposits, withdrawals, and online banking basics.
    • Understanding fees, interest rates, and account maintenance tips.
  • Basic Labor Law (Know Your Rights):
    • An introduction to workers' rights and responsibilities in the workplace, Understanding employment contracts, wages, and working hours.
    • Knowing the basics of fair treatment, discrimination, and workplace safety.
    • How to handie workplace disputes and where to seek help if needed.
  • Basic Reading and Writing Literacy:
    • Improving basic reading comprehension skills for everyday life (e.g., reading instructions, signs, and documents).
    • Writing basic documents such as forms, letters, and emails.
    • Enhancing vocabulary and sentence structure for clear communication.
  • Basic Numeric Literacy:
    • Understanding fundamental math skills (e.g., addition, subtraction, multiplication, division).
    •Applying these skills in everyday situations such as budgeting, shopping, and managing expenses.
    • Learning to read and interpret simple numerical information such as bills, pay slips, and invoices.

This course is ideal for individuals who want to build essential life skills that will help them in both their personal and professional lives. Participants will leave with practical tools to navigate financial services, understand their workplace rights, and improve their literacy and numeracy, all of which can lead to greater independence and empowerment.
